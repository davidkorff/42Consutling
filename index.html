Who We Are

Insurance Technology Specialists

42 Consulting LLC is a technology consulting firm helping insurance organizations move faster and operate smarter. We specialize in the design, integration, and optimization of systems for Managing General Agents (MGAs), carriers, and brokers across Property & Casualty, Excess & Surplus, and Accident & Health lines.

Our mission is simple: design practical solutions for complex problems, and implement them quickly. We eliminate friction so your operations, underwriting, and reporting teams can focus on growth—not system workarounds.
